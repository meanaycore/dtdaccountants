Business Background

Emsini Accounting Practice Trading As Door To Door Accountants, was established in 2007 as a sole trader, and was later registered in 2010. The sole purpose of this institution was/is to create job and training opportunities specifically for historically disadvantaged indivuduals (HDI'S), who have obtained Accounting Qualifications, but were never given an opportunity to do their Articles in the Accounting Institutes, in order to be recognized as registered Accountants of South Africa.


Emsini Accounting Practice is committed in providing full-time and part-time training to those who were unable to do it full-time and also to those who are willing to do it on a fulltime basis. The company believes these individuals have the potential to add significant value to the Accounting profession and to the climate condition of our economy. This belief is in line with the Association for the Advancement of Black Accountants of South Africa (ABASA).

By this, Emsini Accounting Practice is saying, 'let us work together in building a generation that will fulfill their dreams and career objectives, let us be part of this initiative of building solid and sustainable future Accountants of South Africa'.

Vision

Emsini Accounting Practice will be the black assurance and advisory services firm of choice, and add value in the accounting field by increasing the number of black accountants with ethis, people centeredness and innovation.

Mission

We shall deliver the highest quality assurance and advisory services exceeding our clients' expectations, and provide peace of mind. We aim to build our success on four pillars:

  • The success of our clients
  • The success of our people
  • The success of our country
  • The success of our continent

Philosophy

  • We believe in passion for service and strive to create a service culture that permeates our entire firm.
  • We aim to contribute towards the growth and development of our clients, staff, country, and consequently our own growth.
  • We ensure that the firm is representative of unity and share vision amongst management and staff.
  • Growth is possible - using the strengths of partnership rather being defeated by its weakness.

We believe that current and future challenges can be met only though aligning our workforce with the vision of the company. Competency will naturally come from commitment towards the vision of the company. Embracing our culture of entrepreneurship enhancement, and redressing past discrimination through Affirmative Action process.

Values

  • We are committed to make a meaningful contribution to the transformation of our country and to the empowerment of its people, with a particular focus on the development of Black professionals.
  • We strive to recruit the best people available, and invest significantly in training our personnel at all levels.
  • We are committed to sound corporate governance, based on three pillars of strength: Integrity, equity and professionalism.
